About Us

BIOVIRATECH is created with the goal of being a decentralized community-driven project with a true purpose. BIOVIRATECH aims to be the most known and most used cryptocurrency in the world. BIOVIRATECH mission is to bring popular cryptocurrency concepts to the mainstream.

Unlike older, comparable projects, BIOVIRATECH introduces holders to next-gen concepts such as participation rewards, NFTs, decentralized exchanges, and more.
